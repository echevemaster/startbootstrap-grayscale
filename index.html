What is a Fedora Day?

Fedora day is a day where fedora contributors come together to talk and work about related projects of our interest.

This time the talks will be related with Gluster, oVirt, Openshift, Openstack,Docker among others

Argentinean Fedora guys and some speakers of other countries will be in the event with the purpose of get contributors for our project.
